Our Mission

CORWADO is dedicated to empowering rural women through sustainable agribusiness development, creating opportunities for economic growth and community transformation.

We believe in the power of women to drive positive change in their communities through agriculture and entrepreneurship.

Our Impact

  • Empowering rural women through agricultural training
  • Creating sustainable livelihoods through agribusiness
  • Building resilient rural communities

Our Programs

Agricultural Training

Comprehensive training programs in modern agricultural techniques and sustainable farming practices.

Business Development

Supporting women entrepreneurs in developing and scaling their agribusiness ventures.

Community Outreach

Engaging with local communities to promote sustainable agricultural practices and women's empowerment.
