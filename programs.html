Our Programmes

Development, humanitarian, and resilience-building with a focus on women's empowerment and sustainable agriculture

The Consortium of Rural Women for Agribusiness Development Organization (CORWADO) has extensive experience supporting rural communities in South Sudan through a combination of development, humanitarian, and resilience-building interventions, with a specific emphasis on women's empowerment, inclusive economic growth, and sustainable agriculture.

a) Food Security, Food System, Agriculture & Livelihoods

  • • Climate-smart and agroecological crop production
  • • Farmer Field Schools & demonstration plots
  • • Post-harvest handling and storage
  • • Nutrition-sensitive agriculture
  • • Smallholder productivity and income diversification

b) Market Systems Development (MSD), Private Sector Engagement & Agribusiness

  • • Inclusive value chain development (sorghum, groundnuts, horticulture, honey, etc.)
  • • Producer organizations & cooperative strengthening
  • • Business Development Services (BDS)
  • • Private-sector engagement and market linkages
  • • Rural enterprise development and aggregation models

c) Women's Economic Empowerment & Gender Equality

  • • Women-led agribusiness and enterprise support
  • • Gender-transformative programming
  • • Leadership development for women
  • • Women's access to finance and markets
  • • Economic empowerment of female-headed households

d) Youth Livelihoods, Entrepreneurship & Leadership

  • • Youth enterprise development and start-up support
  • • Vocational and entrepreneurship skills training
  • • Youth leadership and civic engagement
  • • Digital inclusion for youth
  • • Employment pathways and green jobs

e) Climate Resilience, DRR & Nature-Based Solutions

  • • Climate Change Adaptation (CCA)
  • • Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR)
  • • Ecosystem restoration & agroforestry
  • • Nature-Based Solutions (NbS)
  • • Community-led climate action and early warning systems

f) Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH)

  • • Access to safe water (community & institutional)
  • • Sanitation and hygiene infrastructure
  • • Hygiene promotion and behavior change
  • • WASH in emergencies
  • • WASH–nutrition–health integration

g) Emergency & Humanitarian Response

  • • Emergency food security and livelihoods
  • • Cash and Voucher Assistance (CVA)
  • • Emergency WASH
  • • Rapid response and early recovery
  • • Shock-responsive programming

h) Peacebuilding, Social Cohesion & Conflict Sensitivity

  • • Community dialogue and peace initiatives
  • • Women- and youth-led peacebuilding
  • • Conflict-sensitive livelihoods
  • • Resource-based conflict prevention
  • • Community reconciliation and trust-building

i) Protection, GBV Prevention & SRHR

  • • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) prevention and response
  • • Women- and girls-friendly spaces
  • • Psychosocial support and referrals
  • • Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR)
  • • Safeguarding and protection mainstreaming

j) Financial Inclusion & Savings Groups

  • • Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLAs)
  • • Financial literacy and business finance skills
  • • Linkages to financial institutions
  • • Green and climate finance at community level
  • • Savings-led resilience models

k) Institutional & Community Capacity Strengthening

  • • Capacity strengthening of cooperatives and RPOs
  • • Governance, leadership, and accountability systems
  • • Civil society and women-led CSO strengthening
  • • Community institutions and local governance
  • • Localization and partner accompaniment

l) Digital Innovation & E-Extension

  • • Digital extension and advisory services
  • • Climate and weather information systems
  • • Digital finance and mobile platforms
  • • Data-driven programming and learning
  • • ICT for agriculture and livelihoods

m) Advocacy, Policy Engagement & Learning

  • • Evidence-based advocacy
  • • Policy dialogue on food systems and gender
  • • Knowledge generation and learning products
  • • Community voices and grassroots advocacy
  • • Contribution to national and regional policy processes
