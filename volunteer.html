Volunteer with CORWADO

Join us in empowering rural women and building resilient communities through sustainable agribusiness.

Why Volunteer With Us

Make a Real Impact

Support women-led agribusiness and help communities achieve food security and economic resilience.

Join a Community

Work alongside local staff, partners, and other volunteers who share your commitment to rural development.

Grow Your Skills

Gain experience in agriculture, WASH, livelihoods, and women’s empowerment in a real-world setting.

Volunteer Opportunities

Choose how you’d like to contribute. We welcome both field and remote volunteers.

Agriculture & Field

Support farming demonstrations, seed distribution, and sustainable agriculture training in communities.

Training & Mentoring

Deliver workshops on business skills, financial literacy, or technical topics relevant to our programs.

Community Outreach

Help with WASH awareness, market linkages, and community mobilization in rural areas.

Remote / Skills-Based

Contribute in communications, fundraising, data, or project support from wherever you are.
