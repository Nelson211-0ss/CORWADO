About CORWADO

Empowering rural women through sustainable agribusiness development since 2010

Our Vision

To create a world where rural women are empowered through sustainable agribusiness, leading to thriving communities and economic independence.

Our Mission

To empower rural women through comprehensive agricultural training, business development, and community engagement, fostering sustainable livelihoods and community transformation.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We believe in the power of women to transform their lives and communities through knowledge and opportunity.

Sustainability

We promote environmentally friendly agricultural practices that ensure long-term success and community resilience.

Community

We foster strong community bonds and collective action to create lasting positive change.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our actions and decisions.

Our Leadership

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Executive Director

Dr. Johnson brings over 15 years of experience in agricultural development and women's empowerment. She holds a Ph.D. in Agricultural Economics and has worked with various international organizations before joining CORWADO.

Mary Okello

Founder & Chairperson

Mary Okello founded CORWADO in 2010 with a vision to transform rural communities through women's empowerment. Her extensive experience in community development and passion for sustainable agriculture has been instrumental in shaping the organization's success.
